Coloring

This process involves going from light to dark, selecting the overall tone or tones you would like in the hair.

Balayage

Involves going lighter and selecting the overall tone or tones you wish to have in your hair.

Color Correction

This process can be anything from removing artificial color OR using multiple coats of color to eliminate "mistake tones". You would then pick a new tone.

Haircut

This service includes a "custom made" precision haircut depending on your specific hair type. It can range from specified layers for baby fine hair to make it feel & appear thicker OR specialized texturizing and shaping for thick hair to make it appear less frizzy & easy to style.

Toner/Touch Up

This process is intended to maintain your new color or an existing color. Touch ups typically occur on a month-to-month basis.

Global Keratin

This product is a formaldehyde FREE smoothing treatment that reminds me of the Brazilian Blowout, but does not hurt me or my clients." It reducing "frizz" and adds shine by up to 90%. I process it in the shampoo bowl, then flat iron into the client's hair, sealing the cuticle and permanently installing the treatment.
