Coloring | $200+


Coloring (Going Darker)

This process involves going from light to dark, selecting the overall tone or tones you would like in the hair.

Balayage (Going Lighter)

This process involves going lighter and selecting the overall tone or tones you wish to have in your hair.

See examples in the gallery

Color Correction | $250+

This process involves a drastic transformation that can take many different specified formulas and also quite some time in the salon.

It can be anything from removing artificial color to achieve a desired shade, OR using multiple coats of color to eliminate "mistake tones" such as orange, yellow, red and green.

Followed by the removal process, you would pick a new tone, (darker, the same level, or lighter.)

  • *grey coverage is standard with all color services

  • *Olaplex is standard with ALL color services

See examples in the gallery

Haircut | $85+

This service includes a "custom made" precision haircut depending on your specific hair type. It can range from specified layers for baby fine hair to make it feel & appear thicker OR specialized texturizing and shaping for thick hair to make it appear less frizzy & easy to style.

Toner/Touch-Up | $85-200+

Toner and touch up prices will occur after your first major service at Sax Hair.

  • Maintaining your new color will ideally run almost half the cost of your original purchase.

  • Maintaining an existing color will run less value than choosing a new color or project.

IE: most of our regular clients will pay $300 for major color every 3-6 months, yet find themselves paying less than half of that on a month to month basis simply for touch ups.

Global Kertain | $300+

This product is a formaldehyde FREE smoothing treatment that reminds me of the Brazilian Blowout, but does not hurt me or my clients." It reducing "frizz" and adds shine by up to 90%. It is safe for fine hair that is damaged, all the way to thick, frizzy & curly hair that needs serious smoothing. This treatment does not edit the curl or wave pattern of the hair.

I process it in the shampoo bowl, then flat iron into the client's hair, sealing the cuticle and permanently installing the treatment.
